Cookie Monster

by: Isaac Baloun

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I was going to make a jumping game. Then I switched over to Cookie Monster because I enjoyed the mechanics of the game. I wanted to make my own version.

2. What I Learned

I learned that I could incorporate new ideas. Some ideas that I received were to add images like make the food cookies and make the circle a monster. There were also other ones, like to make the foods add more points or take away points.

3. Architecture of My Game

There is the title screen. This part tells you how to play the game. There is the game screen where you can play the screen. There is also the end screen so when you reach 200 it tells you that you win. There is the ball which is what you play as. There are food items which gain you points when you run over them.

4. Technical Implementation

I am proud of the fact that there are a set number of food items. Every time you eat one another one spawns. This makes it so you don't have to chase all over the screen.

5. Most Challenging Part

There were many challenges. One of them was the collision box. It was not working and every time I ran into a food nothing would happen. Another was the spawning mechanism. It would not spawn new food when I ran into them.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

I would improve it by making it so that there would be different food items worth different amounts of points. Like you could lose points. If you reached zero points you lost.
